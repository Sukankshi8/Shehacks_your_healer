Your Healer

>To increase your activity:

Set specific goals for your physical activity.
Take the stairs instead of the elevator.
Take a walk on your lunch breaks. Or have “walking meetings” with colleagues at work.
Eat Healthy.
Eat smaller portions.
Select a mix of colorful vegetables each day.
Choose whole grains.
Go easy on fats and oils.
Limit added sugars.
Get plenty of sleep.
Limit alcohol use. Alcohol is high in calories and may worsen health conditions common
among older adults.
Avoid tobacco products. When you quit smoking, you may improve many aspects of
your health and are likely to add years to your life.
