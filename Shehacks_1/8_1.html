Its common for all of us to feel lonely from time to time.
Loneliness doesn't mean that you are alone because,we may
feel lonely even when we surrounded by friends,family,etc.
Some things which can help us to overcome lonliness are:

Be friends with yourself first. Self care is something
which is extremely important for our mental health.
But many times we tend to ignore ourselves and just
focus on other's opinions about us.

Be available so that you can find people with similar
mindset and find a good company.

Join an online community of like minded people so
that you have people to talk to with similar goals
and aspirations.
