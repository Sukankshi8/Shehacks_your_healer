If you can't sleep all night well you need to fix your sleep problem
because sleep is the most important part of our life.
It plays a major role in our focusing power, memorizing
power and our physical health in general.

The the circadian rhythm is the name of our body's internal clock.
It is regulated by two hormones that we produce-
Cortisol and Melatonin. So how can we regulate the release of
these hormones.

- Try to have some sunlight exposure.
- Do some sort of exercise when you wake up.
- Eliminate your caffeine intake after 4 p.m.
- Control your lighting in your room.
- Set up temperature from 15.5 to 20 degree Celsius.
- Do not look at your devices when you are ready to sleep.
